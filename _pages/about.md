I am a Ph.D. student in Informatics at the [Luddy School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering](https://luddy.indiana.edu/about/index.html) at [Indiana University, Bloomington (IU)](https://www.iu.edu/index.html), under the supervision of Prof. [Jisun An](https://jisun.me/) and Prof. [Haewoon Kwak](https://haewoon.io/). Prior to joining IU, I obtained a Ph.D. in Political Science and an M.S. in Data Science from the [University of Rochester](https://www.rochester.edu/), where my M.S. study was supervised by Prof. [Jiebo Luo](https://www.cs.rochester.edu/u/jluo/). I also obtained my M.A. in Public Policy from the [University of Chicago](https://www.uchicago.edu/en) and B.A. in Economics from [Wuhan University](https://www.whu.edu.cn/).

My research interests broadly involve data mining, computational social science, and AI for social good. I am particularly focused on integrating social science knowledge with innovative machine learning and AI techniques to better understand human behavior and advance the development of AI that is safe, fair, and responsible.
